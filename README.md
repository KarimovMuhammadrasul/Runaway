# Runaway
<img width="1600" height="884" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f14e8f6c-3bcc-41fc-9712-2669d0e8239d" />



Runaway is a creative experiment that turns your hands into a musical instrument. Inspired by the vibe of *My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy*, it uses your webcam to track your movements and turn them into sound.

Instead of a boring camera feed, you’ll see your hands as a glowing skeleton against the album's iconic green backdrop—it feels less like an app and more like a living piece of art.

### How to Play
Just click the screen to get started:

* **Left Hand:** Curl your fingers to trigger samples.
* **Right Hand:** Pinch to start a loop, or make a fist to stop it.

### What's Under the Hood
It’s built for the browser using **MediaPipe** for smooth tracking, the **Web Audio API** for the music, and some custom CSS to get that signature crimson-and-gold look. 

Enjoy the music!
